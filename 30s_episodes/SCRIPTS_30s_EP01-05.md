## EP.01: "A Single Dog of Good Fortune" [30s]
**Characters:** Lady Woofington-Barks (Pomeranian, 3 stacked bonnets), Lord Biscuit-Bottoms (Basset Hound, newspaper larger than his entire body)
**Location:** Chintz Regency drawing room, side table stacked 12 high with buttered scones
**Music & SFX:** 
0:00-0:06: Benny Hill fast chase theme
0:06-0:21: Panicked harpsichord trill
0:21-0:30: Dead silence + single crumb drop
SFX: Door explosion, bonnet whoosh, paper rustle, dramatic wheezing inhale

### BEAT SHEET
0:00-0:04: Lady WB explodes through the door at 40mph, top bonnet flies off instantly
0:04-0:13: Skids on polished floor, trips over scone pile, faceplants directly onto Biscuit's newspaper
0:13-0:24: Scrambles up, shakes him by the lapels screaming the news. Biscuit does not move a single muscle.
0:24-0:30: Lady WB freezes staring at camera. Biscuit turns one single page.

### DIALOGUE
NARRATOR: (0:01 deadpan) "It is a truth universally acknowledged that a Pomeranian with gossip will break drywall to deliver it."
NARRATOR: (0:17) "She has five unmarried daughters. He has a newspaper. This is every book ever written."
NARRATOR: (0:27) "Nobody has ever achieved this level of boredom. Not even sloths."
LADY WB: (muffled first through newspaper then screaming) "BISCUIT. THERE IS A CAT. HE CAN AFFORD TUNA EVERY SINGLE DAY."
LORD BISCUIT: (muffled behind paper, zero inflection) "Mmm."

### VISUAL PROMPT ADDITIONS
Slow motion close up on the flying bonnet arcing gracefully across the room. Lady WB's paws skid so fast they leave smoke trails on the wood floor. Biscuit is sunk so deep into the sofa only his ears and newspaper are visible. The page turn is executed with the lazy contempt of a god who has seen everything. Hold perfectly still for 3 full seconds on Lady WB's bulging horrified eyes.

### HOOK & STINGER
[First 2s] DOOR EXPLODES OFF HINGES. POMERANIAN GOES FLYING.
[Final 3s] Dead silence. Page turns. Lady WB starts foaming slightly at the mouth. FREEZE FRAME. TEXT: HE DOES NOT CARE.

## EP.02: "He Dances With NOBODY" [30s]
**Characters:** Lady WB, Lord Biscuit, Lord Darcy Pawsworth (Black Maine Coon, 7ft tall including top hat)
**Location:** Grand ballroom, 47 dogs waltzing badly, chandelier swaying
**Music & SFX:**
0:00-0:11: Rousing orchestral waltz
0:11-0:22: Music cuts completely dead
0:22-0:30: Single sustained sad trombone note
SFX: Coat fabric tearing, snore bubble pop, judgmental cat sniff

### BEAT SHEET
0:00-0:07: Lady WB drags Biscuit across the dance floor by his ear, his paws sliding uselessly
0:07-0:16: Slams him against the wall, points. Darcy leans against a pillar ignoring all of existence
0:16-0:25: Biscuit falls asleep standing up. Giant snore bubble inflates from his nose.
0:25-0:30: Darcy glances directly at them. Snore bubble pops.

### DIALOGUE
NARRATOR: (0:03) "Every ball has one guy. The guy who came just to stand in the corner and judge everyone. This guy is that guy. And he is rich."
NARRATOR: (0:19) "Biscuit has not been awake since 1812. Nobody has noticed."
NARRATOR: (0:27) "He saw that. Oh he absolutely saw that."
LADY WB: (hissing so hard her whiskers flutter) "TEN THOUSAND A YEAR. HE HAS NEVER SMILED. I WILL MARRY A DAUGHTER TO HIM IF IT KILLS US BOTH."
LORD DARCY: (quiet, audible to entire room) "...Dogs."

### VISUAL PROMPT ADDITIONS
Darcy's top hat is so tall it brushes the chandelier crystals. Biscuit's ear stretches 18 inches when pulled. Extreme slow mo close up on Darcy's eyebrow twitching exactly once when the bubble pops. Every other dog in the background has frozen mid-waltz to stare.

### HOOK & STINGER
[First 2s] BASSET HOUND BEING DRAGGED FACE FIRST ACROSS DANCE FLOOR
[Final 3s] Darcy makes unblinking direct eye contact with camera. TEXT: HE JUDGES YOU.

## EP.03: "Tolerable, I Suppose" [30s]
**Characters:** Darcy, Eliza Fetchworth (Border Collie, chewed bonnet, mud boot, elite eyebrow game), Bingley (Golden Retriever grinning so hard his eyes are closed)
**Location:** Ballroom curtain alcove, punch bowl just out of frame
**Music & SFX:**
0:00-0:12: Single tense sustained cello note
0:12-0:21: Record scratch
0:21-0:30: Crickets
SFX: Claw tap, awkward swallow, bonnet string ping

### BEAT SHEET
0:00-0:08: Darcy leans on wall insulting Eliza to Bingley, gesturing lazily across the room
0:08-0:17: Mid-insult he stops. Eliza is standing 12 inches from his face, arms crossed, watching him.
0:17-0:26: Darcy blinks once. Extremely slowly. His eyebrow does not move.
0:26-0:30: Eliza very slowly lifts her paw. Flicks him directly on the nose.

### DIALOGUE
NARRATOR: (0:02) "This is the most famous insult in literature. It is about to go very badly."
NARRATOR: (0:15) "He thought she was on the other side of the room. She was not. She was there the whole time."
NARRATOR: (0:28) "That is the sound of a cat realising he just ruined the next 200 pages."
DARCY: (bored, not looking) "She is tolerable I suppose. Not handsome enough to tempt me."
ELIZA: (dry, right next to his ear) "Thank you. That is so kind."

### VISUAL PROMPT ADDITIONS
When Darcy notices her, his tail twitches so hard it knocks over the entire punch bowl behind him. Nobody acknowledges this. There is one oak leaf stuck in Eliza's bonnet. Darcy's pupils dilate to full black when flicked. Bingley is still grinning, has no idea anything happened.

### HOOK & STINGER
[First 2s] EXTREME CLOSE UP DARCY'S JUDGY EYEBROW
[Final 3s] Nose flick. Darcy freezes. TEXT: OH.

## EP.04: "The Rain Walk Incident" [30s]
**Characters:** Eliza, Darcy, random passing duck
**Location:** Muddy country lane, horizontal rain, useless 1820s umbrella
**Music & SFX:**
0:00-0:09: Pathetic rain patter
0:09-0:22: Pony gallop sound effect
0:22-0:30: Giant wet SPLAT
SFX: Umbrella inside out pop, duck quack, defeated sigh

### BEAT SHEET
0:00-0:07: Eliza tramps through mud, bonnet disintegrating, happily eating blackberries
0:07-0:18: Darcy gallops around the corner on a pony, sees her, panics, tries to u-turn
0:18-0:27: Pony slips. Darcy goes flying. Lands face down in a puddle exactly at her paws.
0:27-0:30: Duck walks past. Stares at him. Quacks once.

### DIALOGUE
NARRATOR: (0:04) "Eliza likes walking in the rain. Darcy likes riding very fast to avoid talking to girls. This was always going to end badly."
NARRATOR: (0:21) "He is the richest man in the county. He has just done a pratfall into mud. In front of the only girl he cares about."
NARRATOR: (0:29) "The duck thinks this is the funniest thing that has ever happened."
DARCY: (muffled through mud) "...Good morning."

### VISUAL PROMPT ADDITIONS
Darcy's top hat comes off mid flight, lands perfectly upside down on the duck's head. Eliza does not flinch. Just stands there, one eyebrow raised, still eating. When Darcy lifts his head out the mud he is still frowning exactly the same way.

### HOOK & STINGER
[First 2s] CAT FLYING THROUGH THE AIR COVERED IN MUD
[Final 3s] Duck with top hat quacks. FREEZE FRAME.

## EP.05: "The First Proposal" [30s]
**Characters:** Darcy, Eliza, Biscuit hiding behind hedge
**Location:** Garden gazebo, overcast, deeply awkward
**Music & SFX:**
0:00-0:17: Dramatic swelling romantic strings
0:17-0:24: Record scratch + glass breaking
0:24-0:30: Silence + very loud sandwich crunch
SFX: Claw clench, gasp, crumbs falling

### BEAT SHEET
0:00-0:10: Darcy paces, vibrates, finally drops to one knee. Wind blows his coat dramatically.
0:10-0:19: Eliza stares at him for 9 full seconds. Then starts snorting laughing.
0:19-0:26: Darcy's entire body goes rigid. Every hair on his tail stands straight up.
0:26-0:30: Very loud crunch from the hedge. Both turn very slowly.

### DIALOGUE
NARRATOR: (0:03) "He practiced this speech 147 times. He is about to get roasted so hard people will quote it in 2025."
NARRATOR: (0:21) "This is the single most rejected man in the entire history of animals."
NARRATOR: (0:28) "And Biscuit is watching. And eating."
DARCY: (strained, dramatic) "I cannot conquer my feelings. You must allow me to tell you how ardently I love you."
ELIZA: (still laughing) "No."

### VISUAL PROMPT ADDITIONS
When Eliza says no, Darcy's eyebrow finally drops. For half a second he looks like he might cry. Then he remembers he is proud. Biscuit is half visible behind the hedge, newspaper propped up for cover, crumbs all over his face. He waves very slowly when they turn.

### HOOK & STINGER
[First 2s] DARCY ON ONE KNEE. EYES FULL OF LOVE.
[Final 3s] Loud crunch. Biscuit waves. TEXT: HE BROUGHT SNACKS.
