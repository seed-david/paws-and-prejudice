## EP.01: "A Single Dog of Good Fortune"
**Characters:** Lady Woofington-Barks (Pomeranian), Lord Biscuit-Bottoms (Basset Hound)
**Music:** Lady Woofington-Barks Overture (#2) → Biscuit-Bottoms Deadpan (#1)
**Location:** Longbourn Drawing Room

NARRATOR: Pomeranians with gossip do not knock.
LADY W-B: *(bursting through door)* BISCUIT. CAT BACHELOR. NETHERFIELD. DRENCHED IN MONEY AND STATUS.
LORD BISCUIT: *(muffled behind newspaper)* Mm.
LADY W-B: *(snaps direct to camera, devastated)* Five daughters. And he just. Turns a page.

## EP.02: "He Dances With NOBODY"
**Characters:** Lady Woofington-Barks, Lord Biscuit-Bottoms, Lord Darcy Pawsworth (tuxedo cat)
**Music:** Full Regency Waltz (#4) cuts hard dead on final beat
**Location:** Netherfield Ballroom

LADY W-B: *(clawing Biscuit's coat, hissing)* There. Darcy. Ten thousand a year. Has not danced once.
DARCY: *(cold, not looking at anyone)* Tolerable room. Insufferable dogs.
LADY W-B: *(vibrating)* He's perfect.
LORD BISCUIT: *(one eye cracks open)* Is he.

## EP.03: "Tolerable, I Suppose"
**Characters:** Lord Darcy Pawsworth, Eliza Fetchworth (Border Collie)
**Music:** The Darcy Stare (#3) sustained cello → Scandal Sting (#11)
**Location:** Ballroom curtain edge

DARCY: *(loud enough for her to hear)* She's fine. Bonnet fought a hedge. Oh. She's listening.
ELIZA: *(ears flat)* I am.
DARCY: *(adjusts cravat)* Saves me repeating it. *(walks away)*
ELIZA: *(snaps to camera)* I have never hated anyone this fast.

## EP.04: "Five Daughters, Zero Plans"
**Characters:** Lady Woofington-Barks, Lord Biscuit-Bottoms
**Music:** Lady Woofington-Barks Overture (#2) spirals higher → cuts dead
**Location:** Longbourn Drawing Room, 2am

LADY W-B: *(pacing, bonnet shredded)* Entail goes to Collins the Chihuahua when you die. We end up in hedges.
LORD BISCUIT: *(behind paper)* I'm fine.
LADY W-B: *(to camera)* Jane won't flirt. Lydia already eloped in her head. Mary is playing piano.
*(offscreen: one very wrong piano note)*
LORD BISCUIT: She's improving.

## EP.05: "The Letter"
**Characters:** Eliza Fetchworth
**Music:** Scandal Sting (#11) 3 violent stabs → dead silence → dragging cello
**Location:** Longbourn rose garden, dawn

NARRATOR: Some letters make you sit in the dirt.
ELIZA: *(reads, dead quiet)* Bingley left. Caroline wrote this. Says Jane isn't pretty enough.
*(pause. A robin lands. Leaves immediately.)*
ELIZA: *(folds letter tiny, locks eyes with camera)* Jane doesn't know yet.
